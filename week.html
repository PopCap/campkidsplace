2020 CAMP WEEKS

OLYMPIC CAMP (June 22nd - 26th):

One of our most popular camps from last summer, Olympic Camp opens our 2020 season, but this time, in the form of a full week! Campers attending this week will get to compete in several Camplympic events that will be even bigger and better than last year. This camp will be filled with competitions of both brain and brawn to challenge campers in new and exciting ways at every turn! From individual achievements to team awards, this will be a summer kickoff you won't want to miss!

CARNIVAL CAMP (June 29th - July 3rd):

The circus returns to town for a second straight summer and tickets are going fast! Word of last year's Camp Circus and Carnival successes spread quickly and the greatest show in Arlington will be filled with more wonder and excitement than its inaugural season. From showmanship, to creating carnival stations, to dunk tanks and more, this packed out week of Camp will provide a lifetime of memories!

RODEO CAMP (July 6th - 10th):

It's time to saddle up and giddy-up to this week of Camp returning for the first time since 2016. Campers will embark on an adventure of a lifetime filled with excitement, bull-riding, and navigation of the American Frontier. Charlie Dallas, the No-Nonsense Sheriff, returns yet again to lead our Camp in standing against lawlessness and the ever-growing unpredictability of the Wild West.

STUDIO CAMP (July 13th - 17th):

Lights! Camera! Action! From Camp Kids Place Got Talent, to team feature films, and the A-Campemy awards, this continues to be one of our most unforgettable camps year after year. Campers attending this camp will have a chance to showcase their talents of any kind, be on the big screen, and win awards with their teams all week long! Campers won't want to miss out on their big break this summer!

HOLIDAY CAMP (July 20th - 24th):

Merry NewFourThanksgivEastBirthmas from Camp Kids Place 2020! We love each of the holidays so much at Camp Kids Place, that we wanted a chance to celebrate some of our favorites with you this summer! Holiday Camp will truly be an incomperable experience. From Christmas in July to Teamsgiving meals to Camp Kids Place National Birthday Day, this week will be a conglomeration of all of the most wonderful times of the year!

CIVILIZATIONS CAMP (July 27th - 31st):

A journey through time amidst the major civilizations of history is merely where this week of Camp begins. Each team will have a chance to create its own civilization and compete in all manner of activities throughout the course of this varied and spirited camp. From there we'll take simply take a trip through our time machine and see what we see! It's new, untested technology, but we're sure it will work perfectly! Besides, what's adventure without a few bumps in the road...?

SUPER CAMP (August 3rd - 7th):

Calling all campers! Super Camp returns and the universe needs YOU! From superhero training, to super activities, and incredible creations, you'll be sure to get a call from CKP Hero Headquarters to join the Campvenger force. Camp Kids Place needs more heroes to protect against jealous villains who would seek its ruin! This weeklong training and, not to mention, fun program will certainly deliver!

BOOT CAMP (August 10th - 14th):

It's time to get our boots back on the ground at Camp Kids Place as this classic camp returns to action. Campers will undergo training and prep in anticipation of the Great American Shaving Cream Battle, Water Wars, and many other events that only the Camp Kids Place troops can handle! We can't wait for the new recruits to arrive!
